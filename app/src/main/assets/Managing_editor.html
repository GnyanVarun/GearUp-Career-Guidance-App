Introduction

Managing editors review, revise, and proofread written works for publication. People in this position are also responsible for training and editing writers, polishing and proofreading written work, and ensuring that all media (such as images and videos) included with the writing is properly sourced. Managing editors are in a leadership role, but they still report to the editor-in-chief of any publication. Managing editors work in a number of environments, including newsrooms and magazine offices, but they may also work from home on a freelance basis as independent contractors.

What do they do?

  • Editing and Rewriting Copy
  • Approve Story Pitches
  • Write Original Content
  • Brainstorm Story Ideas
  • Assign Stories to Writers
  • Create Marketing Strategies
  • Hire and Train Writers

Key skills required

  • Mastery of English
  • Editing skills
  • Leadership skills
  • Sales and marketing
